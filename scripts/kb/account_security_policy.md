# Account Security and Access Policy

## Password reset requests

Password resets are handled through the customer portal's self-service flow at the login
page (click "Forgot password"). This helpdesk does not have the ability to reset passwords
or access account credentials. If the customer cannot access the self-service reset (e.g.
they no longer have access to their registered email), escalate to a human account security
specialist with reason "account access — email change required".

## Suspicious activity on an account

If a customer reports orders they did not place, charges they do not recognise, or believes
their account has been compromised:
1. Look up any order IDs they provide to confirm they exist in the system.
2. Open a support ticket via take_action (action_type "create_ticket",
   reason "suspected account compromise") to flag the account for the security team.
3. Advise the customer to change their password immediately via the customer portal.
4. Escalate to a human security specialist if the customer reports multiple fraudulent
   orders or requests account suspension.

## Account closure requests

Account closures are handled by the account management team, not this helpdesk. Escalate
to a human with reason "account closure request".

## Two-factor authentication (2FA) issues

2FA setup and recovery are handled via the customer portal. This system does not have
access to 2FA codes or backup codes. Escalate to a human if the customer is locked out
due to 2FA.

## Data and privacy requests (GDPR / CCPA)

Requests to access, export, or delete personal data under GDPR, CCPA, or similar
regulations must be escalated to a human compliance specialist with reason "data subject
request". Do not attempt to fulfil these requests through this helpdesk.
